Executive Summary

Key Finding

On average, it takes 4.8 touches (4.3 calls + 0.4 SMS) to convert a lead into a first appointment. The optimal contact window is 14-59 days before auction, accounting for 59% of successful conversions.

Data Coverage

93.6% of converted leads have auction date data, enabling precise timing analysis. 99.3% have Twilio activity records for complete touch tracking.

How Many Touches Does It Take?

Quick Wins (1-2 touches)

44.2% of leads convert within 1-2 touches. These are likely highly motivated sellers responding to urgency.

Persistence Pays

9.3% require 10+ touches. Don't give up on leads - nearly 1 in 10 conversions come from persistent follow-up.

When to Contact: Timing is Everything

Strategic Recommendations

1. Start Early, Peak at 30 Days

Begin outreach 60+ days before auction. Increase cadence at 30 days. Maximum effort at 14-29 days before auction.

2. Plan for 5 Touches

Budget and schedule for an average of 5 contact attempts per lead. 44% will convert in 1-2, but 56% need more persistence.

3. Prioritize Calls

Voice calls are 10x more effective than SMS for this audience. AI voice agents are performing well at booking appointments.

4. Don't Abandon After 5

Nearly 10% of conversions happen after 10+ touches. Maintain contact cadence for the full lead lifecycle.
